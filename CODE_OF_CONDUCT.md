# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free, and collaborative environment.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical content, evidence, and outcomes.
- Accept and provide feedback professionally.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, abuse, or intimidation.
- Discriminatory language or personal attacks.
- Sharing private or sensitive information without consent.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this policy.
